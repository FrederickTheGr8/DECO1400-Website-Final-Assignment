Soviet Space Program Tour

×

See the historical Space Race between the USSR and the United States from the perspective of the USSR.

Duration : 4 hours

  • Meet cosmonauts from the Soviet space program before they embark on dangerous missions
  • Witness the launches of the first humans to enter space and orbit Earth
  • See the innards of the Buran spacecraft, before it was destroyed in 2002 hangar collapse
  • Observe the launch of Sputnik-1, the first artificial earth satellite
  • See the launch of Luna 2, the Soviet Cosmic Rocket that impacted the moon in 1959

The space program of the USSR was helped dearly by captured scientists out of Germany after the end of WW2. While the Americans had a single agency coordinating their eforts in the space race (NASA), the USSR had several bureaus, often competing with eachother on designs and funding. Due to the secrecy of the Communist government, people in the Soviet Union were often not up to date with the achievements and also failures of the Soviet Space Program. They achieved many great things though, the first human in space and Earth orbit being Yuri Gagarin in the Vostok 1, the first woman in space Valentina Tereshkova in the Vostok 6, the first space walk by Alexei Leonov on the Voshkod 2, the first Moon impact with the Luna 2 and the first image of the far side of the Moon by Luna 3. As well as many other achievements.
